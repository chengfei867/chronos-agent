### Key insight this round

The **LangGraph checkpointer is the closest competitor** and also the **closest friend**:
- It offers primitive state save/restore (half the work of fork)
- But locked to LangGraph ecosystem; no UI; no diff; no cross-framework
- Strategy: be to LangGraph checkpointer what `git` is to `tar`
